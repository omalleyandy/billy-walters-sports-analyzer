# Billy Walters Betting Methodology

Comprehensive guide to the Billy Walters analytical framework.

## Core Philosophy

Information Edge + Statistical Modeling + Disciplined Bankroll Management

## Edge Detection Thresholds

| Edge | Confidence | Kelly | Win Rate | Recommendation |
|------|-----------|-------|----------|---|
| 7+ points | Maximum | 5% | 77% | MAX BET |
| 4-7 points | Strong | 3% | 64% | STRONG |
| 2-4 points | Moderate | 2% | 58% | MODERATE |
| 1-2 points | Lean | 1% | 54% | LEAN |
| <1 point | Minimal | 0% | 50% | NO PLAY |

## Success Metrics

**Primary**: ROI (not win percentage)
- Professional: +2-3% ROI
- Break-even: 52.4% win rate at -110 vig

**Secondary**: ATS (Against The Spread)
- Good: 55%+, Excellent: 58%+

**Tertiary**: CLV (Closing Line Value)
- Professional: +1.5 average
- Elite: +2.0+ average

## The Billy Walters Approach

1. Find Information Edges
2. Quantify Value (7+, 4-7, 2-4, 1-2, <1)
3. Size Bets Properly (Kelly Criterion)
4. Track Performance (ROI and CLV)
5. Make Adjustments (Learn from CLV)
