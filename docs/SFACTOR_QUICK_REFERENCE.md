# Billy Walters S and W Factor - Quick Reference Card

**Formula: 5 Factor Points = 1 Spread Point**

---

## S-FACTORS (Situational)

### Turf
- Same turf: **+1 Visitor**
- Opposite turf: **+1 Home**

### Division/Conference
- Same division: **+1 Visitor**
- Different conference: **+1 Home**

### Night Games
- Thursday night: **+2 Home**
- Sunday night: **+4 Home**
- Monday night: **+2 Home**
- Saturday night: **0**

### Monday Night Recovery
- Home off MNF home: **0**
- Home off MNF away: **+4 Opponent**
- Away off MNF home: **+6 Team**
- Away off MNF away: **+8 Team**

### Bye Week (by Team Quality)

| Quality | Home | Away |
|---------|------|------|
| Below Avg | +4 | +5 |
| Average | +5 | +6 |
| Great | +7 | +8 |

### Time Zones

**10:00 AM Games:**
- West Coast team: **+2 Opponent**
- Mountain team: **+1 Opponent**

**Night Games:**
- East Coast team: **+6 Opponent**
- Central team: **+3 Opponent**
- Mountain team: **+1 Opponent**

### Bounce-Back
- Lost by 19-28 pts: **+2 Team**
- Lost by 29+ pts: **+4 Team**

### Other Schedule
- 3rd away in 4 games: **+2 Home**
- Off OT at home: **+4 Team**
- Off OT away: **+2 Team**
- 2nd consecutive 2+ TZ: **+2 Home**

## W-FACTORS (Weather)

### Temperature (Warm Team to Cold)

| Temp | Spread Adj (Home) |
|------|-------------------|
| 35°F | +0.25 |
| 30°F | +0.50 |
| 25°F | +0.75 |
| 20°F | +1.00 |
| 15°F | +1.25 |
| ≤10°F | +1.75 |

### Temperature (Dome Team to Cold)

| Temp Range | Spread Adj (Home) |
|------------|-------------------|
| 30-20°F | +0.25 |
| 20-10°F | +0.50 |
| 10-5°F | +0.75 |

### Precipitation
- Rain: **+0.25 Visitor**
- Hard rain: **+0.75 Visitor**
- Snow: **Variable**

### Wind
- >20 mph: **Variable** (team-dependent)

## CALCULATION EXAMPLE

**Game: Bills @ Lions, Week 12**

**Lions (Home):**
- Bye week (Great team): +7 points
- Spread adjustment: +1.40

**Bills (Away):**
- No major factors: 0 points

**Net Advantage: Lions +1.40 points**

## TYPICAL RANGES

**Normal game:** 0-5 S-factor points (0-1 spread pt)  
**Significant situation:** 5-10 points (1-2 spread pts)  
**Rare stacking:** 10-18 points (2-3.6 spread pts)

**Billy Walters 2022-23 Average: 3.2 S-factor points per team per week (0.64 spread pts)**
